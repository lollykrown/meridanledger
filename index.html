About Us

Numbers you can trust. Advice you can act on.

Meridian Ledger & Co. was founded on a simple conviction: every business deserves the financial clarity that large corporations take for granted. For more than fifteen years, our chartered accountants have helped founders, family businesses, and growing companies keep accurate books, stay fully compliant, and plan with confidence.

We combine rigorous accounting standards with modern cloud tools, so you always know exactly where your business stands — and where it can go next. No jargon, no surprises, no missed deadlines.
